# FAQ #### Where are my data and result files stored? Every dataset and every result is stored in a subfolder of your project. E.g. for project p1001 the folder is accessible at. [http://fgcz-sushi.uzh.ch/projects/p1001/](http://fgcz-sushi.uzh.ch/projects/p1001/). Your dataset folder will hold, the data, script, parameter, and log files that fully document your dataset. You can download the entire folder with your browser of choice. For advanced download options see [http://fgcz-intranet.uzh.ch/tiki-index.php?page=bioinformatics.gStore.get_data](http://fgcz-intranet.uzh.ch/tiki-index.php?page=bioinformatics.gStore.get_data.) Access requires your FGCZ username/password (see [http://fgcz-bfabric.uzh.ch/bfabric/](http://fgcz-bfabric.uzh.ch/bfabric/)). Data and results are securely and redundantly stored. "
