# Home Get started at [fgcz-sushi.uzh.ch](http://fgcz-sushi.uzh.ch), go to **Today's Menu** and select your project number and then **DataSet** to start analyzing data or viewing analysis results. ## DataSet Listing The available DataSets are listed as a tree structure and as a simple table with the newest DataSets at the top. <img src=Images/datasetListing.png width="600"> ## DataSet Inspection Click on a Dataset to view the details. The **Samples** table shows the meta-information about the samples and reference to data files and links to browsable reports (if available). <img src=Images/datasetDetails.png width="600"> ## DataSet Analysis At the bottom of every DataSet page there is the list of **Applications** that are compatible with the DataSet. Click on your Application of choice for the analysis of the DataSet. A second page will show up and let you enter the analysis options. Click on **Next**, and verify your settings on the upcoming page. If everything is correct **Submit** your analysis job. <img src=Images/sample.png> "
